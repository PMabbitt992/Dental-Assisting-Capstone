The Dental Assisting program participates in HOSA (Health Occupations Students of America). The program competes in a variety of competitions to ready and test its students.

HOSA's mission is to promote careers in the health industry and to enhance the quality of healthcare delivered to patients. HOSA provides leadership development, motivation, and recognition for students pursuing a career in health professions.

HOSA has over 245,000 members
