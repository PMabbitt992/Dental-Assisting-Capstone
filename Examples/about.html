PROGRAM DESCRIPTION
—
This two-year program prepares students to assist medical professionals with dental examinations, sterilize and prepare instruments polish teeth, take and develop X-rays, and take impressions of teeth. West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.
