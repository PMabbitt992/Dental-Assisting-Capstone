PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

—
This two-year program prepares students to assist medical professionals with dental examinations, sterilize and prepare instruments polish teeth, take and develop X-rays, and take impressions of teeth. The program goes over a variety of knowledge in the goal of preparing student for the workforce, including dental history, practices, materials, anatomy, technology, patient care, and much more. Dental Assisting is taught by Dorothy Witman.

West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.
