Welcome to the West-MEC Dental Assisting!

Welcome to our program!

In Dental Assisting, we prepare students who are looking for careers in dental profesisons by teaching them everything they need to be ready for the workforce.

Western Maricopa Education Center is a public school district dedicated to providing students with a career and technical educaton. Students should be prepared to enter the workforce or continue furthering their education upon graduation, and West-MEC helps provide those skills.
